Our Privacy Commitment

AvoPress ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data ‚Äî or rather, the complete lack thereof.

We believe that what happens on your iPhone should stay on your iPhone. That's why AvoPress processes everything locally and never transmits your data anywhere.

Data We Collect

None.

AvoPress does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or any other form of data from you or your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access anything on your device.

Photos Library Access

AvoPress requests access to your Photos library for the following purposes only:

  • To scan and display your photos and videos within the app
  • To read file sizes and metadata for compression estimates
  • To save compressed copies back to your Photos library

This access is used solely to provide the app's core functionality. Your media files are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

What We Don't Use

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks
  • No third-party services
  • No cloud storage or servers
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No internet connection required for core functionality

Local Processing

All compression and conversion processes happen entirely on your iPhone. Your photos and videos never leave your device. The compressed files are saved directly to your Photos library, just like any other photo or video you take with your camera.

Children's Privacy

AvoPress does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect data, there are no special provisions needed for children's data.

Changes to This Policy
