Frequently Asked Questions

How does AvoPress work?
AvoPress scans your photo library, lets you select which files to compress, and processes them locally on your device. You can adjust quality settings, resolution, and format to find the perfect balance between size and quality.
Will I lose quality when compressing?
AvoPress gives you full control over compression settings. You can preview the quality before processing and choose settings that work best for you. Most users find significant size savings with minimal visible quality loss.
Are my photos uploaded to the cloud?
No. All processing happens entirely on your iPhone. Your photos and videos never leave your device. We don't have servers, accounts, or any way to access your media.
What happens to my original files?
AvoPress saves compressed copies to your Photos library. Your original files remain untouched until you choose to delete them manually. You're always in control.
Which formats are supported?
AvoPress supports all common photo formats (HEIC, JPEG, PNG) and video formats (MOV, MP4, HEVC). You can convert between formats and choose codecs like H.264 or HEVC for videos.
Why does the app need Photos access?
AvoPress requires access to your Photos library to scan for media files and save compressed versions back to your library. This permission is used only for these purposes â€” no data is collected or shared.
